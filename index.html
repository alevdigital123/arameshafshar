My approach

Built on connection, not blame.

My work with couples is rooted in attachment theory and Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT) — a well-researched, evidence-based approach originally developed by Dr. Sue Johnson to help partners understand the emotions and needs driving their cycle of conflict.

Rather than getting stuck on who's right, we slow things down to see the pattern you're both caught in — and the softer feelings underneath the frustration. From there, we create new ways of reaching each other that feel safe, honest, and genuinely connecting.

My style is warm, collaborative, and direct. I'm here to help you not just understand what's happening between you, but to create real, lasting change.
